About Liminal Weather

Welcome to Liminal Weather, a specialized consultancy that exists at the threshold between complex weather science and accessible understanding. We focus on explaining weather phenomena and technical data to non-technical clients, operating in the very niche of the niche.

We are a no-bullshit company that works exclusively with open source software and open source data. We believe in complete transparency about our methodology and host all our code on GitHub.

Our Services

We specialize in translating complex meteorological and environmental data into clear, actionable insights for decision-makers across various industries.

Weather Data Analysis

Transform raw meteorological data into meaningful insights. We work with ensemble forecasts, climate projections, and real-time weather observations to provide clear, actionable information.

Custom Visualizations

Create compelling visual narratives from your data. From interactive dashboards to static reports, we make complex weather patterns and trends accessible to any audience.

Technical Translation

Bridge the communication gap between technical teams and stakeholders. We explain complex meteorological concepts in plain language without losing scientific accuracy.

Open Source Solutions

Develop transparent, reproducible analysis workflows using open source tools. All methodologies are documented and code is made available for full transparency.
